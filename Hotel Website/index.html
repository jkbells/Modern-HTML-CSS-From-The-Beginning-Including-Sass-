About Our Hotel

The HistoryOf Our Hotel

The original Atlantis Resort opened its doors in 1994 and quickly became an iconic landmark in the Bahamas. However, the story of Atlantis on Paradise Island began even earlier. In the 1960s, a South African hotel magnate named Sol Kerzner purchased the land where Atlantis Resort now stands. He envisioned creating a grand resort complex that would attract tourists from around the world.
